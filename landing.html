How it works
Get started in minutes

No crypto experience needed. Three steps and you're live.

1
Sign in with email or connect wallet
Enter your email for a secure browser wallet, or connect MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase, Rabby or Circle wallet. NAN supports all EVM-compatible wallets on Arc Testnet.
2
Get free testnet tokens
Visit faucet.circle.com, select Arc Testnet, paste your address and claim free USDC & EURC. USDC is also Arc's native gas token.
3
Explore the full DeFi stack
Send USDC to any address, swap to EURC via StableFX, lend for 4.80% APY (Band oracle powered), bridge cross-chain via CCTP, or register your .arc name from 2 USDC.
